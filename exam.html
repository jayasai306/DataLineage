Data Lineage

A data life cycle that includes the data's origins and where it moves over time. End users are able to trace back dat ain their reports to the original source through all different layers and transformation and presentation steps.

ETL Conversion

ETL technologies are an essential part of data warehousing and data integration projects. Occasionally, a need arises to convert systems from ETL (Extract-Transform-Load) style to ELT (Extract-Load-Transform) style.

Impact Analysis

IT needs to know which reports and users will be affected when a change is made to one of the source systems.
